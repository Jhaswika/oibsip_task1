Vignan Institute of Information Technology

Vignan's Institute of Information Technology was established by Dr Lavu Rathaiah in the year 2002 in Visakhapatnam. The institute is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and is affiliated to the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK). Vignan's Institute of Information Technology is recognised as 8th Top Emerging Engineering College in India and 7th Top Engineering College in Andhra Pradesh. With more than 4,500 registered faculty members and more than 370 students, the institute offers 18 courses including BTech, MTech, MCA and MBA. There are a total of 10 departments in the institute including Civil Engineering, Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering etc. The institute has a separate central training and placement cell to conduct campus interviews for placements. The campus of the institute is spread across an area of 17 acre and featured all the basic facilities to its students including well-equipped labs, WiFi, library, canteen, medical and hostel facilities.
